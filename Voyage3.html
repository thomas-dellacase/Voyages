Rikugi-en Gardens are a Tokyo metropolitan park in Bunkyō-ku.

The name Rikugi-en means Garden of the Six Principles, referring to the six elements in waka poetry, based on the traditional division of Chinese poetry into six categories.

The gardens consist of a small pond, trees, and a hill.
